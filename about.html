How CleanSpin started

Founded in 2024, CleanSpin started with a simple observation: laundry is a repetitive chore that consumes hours of valuable weekend time. We wanted to build a service that didn't just wash clothes, but actually cared for them, while making the booking process as easy as ordering food or hailing a cab.

Today, we service thousands of homes across the metropolitan areas, offering everything from wash & fold packages to specialized couture gown dry cleaning, without sacrificing our commitment to eco-friendly practices.

Our Mission

To eliminate laundry stress entirely by providing premium, convenient, and highly professional clothing care services.

Our Vision

To build the world's most sustainable on-demand cleaning grid, powered by smart hubs and zero-emission delivery valets.

Our Values

Uncompromising quality, environmental integrity, client first support, and absolute transparency in pricing.
