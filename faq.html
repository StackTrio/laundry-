Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common queries regarding our premium on-demand laundry system.

Simply go to our booking portal, fill in your contact information, address, and select a preferred date and time slot. Our valet driver will arrive at your address with clean bags to collect your garments. Once cleaned at our hub, they are returned to your door within 24 to 48 hours depending on your chosen plan.

We do not have a hard minimum order size. However, orders below ₹500 will incur a flat shipping and handling valet fee of ₹50. Orders above ₹500 receive absolutely free pickup and return delivery.

CleanSpin is committed to sustainability. We use high-quality biodegradable liquid detergents that are hypoallergenic, dye-free, and perfume-free. They are extremely soft on delicate fabrics and safe for sensitive skin types.

Yes, once your booking is collected, you'll receive a message receipt with a tracking code. You can enter this code in our Order Tracking page to see exactly what phase (Received, Washing, Drying, Out for Delivery) your order is currently undergoing.

All delicate clothes (silks, wools, designer labels) are processed under our Premium Dry Cleaning plan. They undergo strict separate inspection, stain pre-treatment, and are cleaned using gentle, non-aqueous solvent extraction cycles that protect colors and garment fibers.

Although damage or loss is extremely rare due to our barcode inventory system, all orders are 100% insured up to 10 times the cleaning cost of the garment, ensuring complete peace of mind.
